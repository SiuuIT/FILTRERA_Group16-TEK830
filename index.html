Aligning FILTRERA with IKEA's principles

Usability:

FILTRERA is designed for managers and supervisors within IKEA’s operations. These users need a simple and effective way to view and analyze safety incidents and risks in daily tasks. By enabling clear visualization of safety data, improvement areas can be identified quickly to support better decision-making and prevent accidents.

Feasibility:

FILTRERA is an app developed in flutter, with the help of a python filtering backend utilizing; Pandas, NumPy, RapidFuzz and OpenPyXL. The technology is accessible and scalable allowing for smooth implementation within IKEA’s internal systems.

Viability:

FILTRERA aligns closely with IKEA’s focus on employee safety, continuous improvement, and data-driven decision-making. It supports IKEA’s core values of creating a safe and sustainable work environment.

Sustainability:

By enabling faster identification and resolution of safety risks, FILTRERA helps reduce workplace accidents. This minimizes sick leaves and costs, while increasing employee satisfaction. We have also considered potential risks such as exclusion of non-technical users, and misinterpretation of collected data—and we are actively working to design an inclusive, secure, and user-friendly interface.
