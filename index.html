Aligning FILTRERA with IKEA's principles

Usability:

FILTRERA is designed for managers and supervisors within IKEA’s operations, particularly in warehouse, logistics, and production environments. These users need a simple and effective way to view and analyze safety incidents and risks in their daily work. By enabling clear visualization of safety data, improvement areas can be identified quickly to support better decision-making and prevent accidents.

Feasibility:

The technology behind FILTRERA is a web-based platform using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with the help of; Pandas, NumPy, RapidFuzz, and OpenPyXL, to handle data processing and analysis. The technology is accessible, secure, and scalable, allowing for smooth implementation within IKEA’s internal systems.

Viability:

FILTRERA aligns closely with IKEA’s focus on employee safety, continuous improvement, and data-driven decision-making. It supports IKEA’s core values of creating a safe and sustainable work environment for all, while also contributing to operational efficiency and quality.

Sustainability:

By enabling faster identification and resolution of safety risks, FILTRERA helps reduce workplace accidents, which minimizes sick leave and cost, while increasing employee satisfaction. We have also considered potential risks such as data privacy, exclusion of non-technical users, and misinterpretation of collected data—and we are actively working to design an inclusive, secure, and user-friendly interface.
