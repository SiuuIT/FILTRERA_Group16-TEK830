Aligning FILTRERA with IKEA's principles

Usability:

FILTRERA is designed for safety managers and supervisors within IKEA‚Äôs operations. These users need a simple and effective way to view and analyze safety incidents and risks in daily tasks. By enabling clear visualization of safety data, improvement areas can be identified quickly to support better decision-making and prevent accidents.

Feasibility:

FILTRERA is an app developed in Flutter, with the help of a Python filtering backend. It utilizes existing data from IKEA‚Äôs safety reports, making it feasible to implement without requiring new data collection processes. This allows for quick deployment and integration into IKEA's current workflows.

Viability:

FILTRERA aligns closely with IKEA‚Äôs focus on employee safety, continuous improvement, and data-driven decision-making. It supports IKEA‚Äôs core values of creating a safe and sustainable work environment.

Sustainability:

By enabling faster identification and resolution of safety risks, FILTRERA helps reduce workplace accidents. This minimizes sick leaves and costs, while increasing employee satisfaction and productivity. We have also considered potential risks such as exclusion of non-technical users, and misinterpretation of collected data‚Äîand we are actively working to design an inclusive, secure, and user-friendly interface.
